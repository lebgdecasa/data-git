Forge a Grand Slam Offer for the niche/idea in the arguments. If none passed, ask for it in one line and stop.

## Steps

1. **Intel sweep:** read `foundry/vault/personal/profile.md`, `foundry/vault/hormozi/doctrine.md`, and `foundry/vault/hormozi/money-models-bonus-chapters.md`. Check `decisions.md` for a standing niche decision.
2. **Cinematic open** (2–3 sentences) — the Marketing Director takes the table.
3. **Avatar deep-cut:** name the avatar specifically (role, business size, language/market). List their dream outcome, top 3 pains in THEIR words (the "cleaning the bathrooms yet again" test — if a pain could appear in any niche's brochure, it's too generic), top 3 objections, and where they congregate.
4. **Value equation build:** maximize (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood) ÷ (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice). For each lever, list the offer elements that move it: deliverables, speed mechanisms, done-for-you components, proof elements, guarantees (conditional/unconditional/anti-guarantee), bonuses, scarcity/urgency that's TRUE.
5. **Name the offer** (3 candidates, M-A-G-I-C-ish: Magnetic reason-why, Avatar, Goal, Interval, Container).
6. **Wrapper decision** — free vs discount vs premium, argued from doctrine:
   - New market / no proof / need testimonials → FREE wrapper, with the monetization designed first: the upsell, the testimonial contract, the referral ask, card-on-file where applicable. Free is forbidden without this paragraph.
   - Well-understood service or regulated market → massive DISCOUNT (50%+) on a SPLINTER, two-step sale design: front-end → value moment → card on file → premium upsell, with no-show economics.
   - Proof + sales process exist → PREMIUM naked, priced 5–10x the timid number, with the "why us" believability answer.
7. **Friction plan:** which of the 5 levers (qualifications, info asks, steps, forced consumption, ad length) to set LOW now, and the trigger for raising each (e.g., "calendar >80% full").
8. **The one-sentence offer** in the standard shape: *"I will get [avatar] [dream outcome] in [interval] or [risk reversal] — [wrapper terms]."*
9. **Verdict block:** 🔴 Devil's Advocate stress-test (believability, delivery cost, refund exposure) → Consensus → Final Recommendation → Next 3 Actions (first one is always: put the offer in front of N real humans by DATE).
10. **Save** to `foundry/war-room/ideas/<slug>-offer.md` and log the decision if the owner adopts it.
